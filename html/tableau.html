Chart Explanations

Profits in Each of the 50 States

This chart shows the profits in each of the 50 states using the given data. Profits are highest in California with New York as the second highest. Texas has the lowest profit with -25,729.

Profits per Month in California

This chart shows the profits per month from January 2013 to December 2014 in California - the state with the highest profit from the previous chart. California's profits were the highest in November of 2013.

Profits per Category in California

This chart shows the profits per category in California. The categories are furniture, office supplies, and technology. The category with the highest profits in California is office supplies.

Sales Forecast for Office Supplies in California

This chart shows the sales forecast for office supplies in California from December 2014 to November 2015 given the actual office supply sales in California from January 2011 to November 2014. Based on the data, the sales for office supplies are expected to increase.
